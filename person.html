What is a PERSON token?

We believe that everyone holds a value and our ultimate asset is human's attention. Without people there would be no value in the world. Our Platform is the first one in the world that lets people easily issue and manage their personal tokens, which are the tool of finding their real market value, creating a network effect and holding their value in their own hands.

PERSON is a token created to provide liquidity to the majority of personal tokens markets. It creates an alternative market with a fix price of 1:1, where every PERSON token, can be exchanged for any personal tokens from the PERSON POOL. Every time some get tokenized, they contribute 1% (210 000 tokens) of their tokens to the PERSON POOL. It's a liquidity pool, that thanks to 1:1 ratio will create arbitrage opportunities and create more volume.

You can buy PERSON tokens on Kanga Exchange.
